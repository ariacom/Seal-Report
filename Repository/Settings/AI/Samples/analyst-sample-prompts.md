# Sample prompts — Data Analyst
# One prompt per line. Blank lines and lines starting with # are ignored.

# --- Data questions ---
What were total sales last month?
Show me the top 10 customers by revenue
Which products are selling the most this year?
What is the sales trend over the last 6 months?
Compare sales across regions for this quarter

# --- Summaries ---
Give me an executive summary of the sales overview report
Summarize the key findings from the [report name] report
What are the main trends in the [report name] data?

# --- Analysis ---
Which category has the highest growth compared to last year?
Are there any anomalies in the latest [report name] results?
What does the data say about [business question]?
